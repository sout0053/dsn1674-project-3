Fast. Friendly. Fresh.

Hao Sushi is a newly opened all you can eat sushi restaurant. We offer lots of amazing food. With over 180 items, there is something for everyone. Give it a try, you will like it!

Menu

Rolls

We have a wide variety of sushi rolls ranging from the Classic Roll (Tuna, avacado, cucumber) California Rolls (Crab, avocado, cucumber) and a variety of spicy rolls such as the Gazilla Roll (Shrimp, avacado, cream cheese).

Ramen

As well as a number of different Ramen dishes, we serve hot soups and wonton as well.

Drinks

We offer a wide variety of drinks to go with your meal, ranging from water, hot green tea, soft drinks and sake.
